What to Know Before You Book

BOOKING

Policy

✦

DEPOSITS

A non-refundable deposit is required to secure your appointment. This goes toward your total service cost.

CANCELLATIONS

Please cancel at least 48 hours in advance. Late cancellations will result in forfeiture of your deposit.

LATENESS

A grace period of 15 minutes is allowed. After that your appointment may be cancelled and deposit forfeited.

HAIR PREP

Please arrive with clean, detangled, and stretched hair. An additional fee may apply for hair prep services.

LOCATION

Location details are sent upon booking confirmation. Mobile services available — inquire at time of booking.

PAYMENT

We accept Cash App, Zelle, and cash. Full balance is due at the time of service. No exceptions.
